Latest Sports News

Stephen Curry Joins Davidson College as Assistant GM

Golden State Warriors guard and four-time NBA champion, Stephen Curry, has joined his alma mater, Davidson College, as an assistant general manager for its basketball teams. This marks the first time an active major professional athlete in the U.S. has taken on such a role with a college team. Curry will continue to play for the Warriors while supporting Davidson's basketball programs.

Seattle Seahawks Trade DK Metcalf to Pittsburgh Steelers

The Seattle Seahawks have traded star wide receiver DK Metcalf to the Pittsburgh Steelers in exchange for a 2025 second-round draft pick. This move follows Metcalf's trade request and signifies a significant shift in the Seahawks' roster strategy.

Yankees' Ace Gerrit Cole to Undergo Tommy John Surgery

New York Yankees' star pitcher, Gerrit Cole, is scheduled to undergo Tommy John surgery on Tuesday. This procedure will sideline him for an extended period, impacting the Yankees' pitching lineup for the upcoming season.

Sam Darnold Signs with Seattle Seahawks

Quarterback Sam Darnold has agreed to a three-year, $100.5 million deal with the Seattle Seahawks. This acquisition aims to strengthen the Seahawks' offense for the upcoming NFL season.
